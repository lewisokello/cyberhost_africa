About Us

Our Story

At Cyber Host Africa, we’re on a mission to bridge the digital divide by delivering fast, reliable internet and next-generation technology solutions to communities across Africa — starting with the most remote and underserved areas. Founded with a bold vision, Cyber Host leverages cutting-edge satellite technology (including Starlink) to provide affordable, scalable, and accessible internet services where traditional ISPs fall short. We go beyond connectivity by offering technical support, web hosting, and an AI-powered Learning Management System (LMS) tailored for schools and training institutions.

We believe that access to technology is a right, not a privilege. That’s why we're committed to empowering youth, businesses, and institutions through digital inclusion, technical training, and innovative platforms that drive economic opportunity and sustainable growth. Our team combines deep expertise in IT infrastructure, cloud services, and customer success to ensure our clients are always supported — whether they're launching a website, managing a school, or running a growing enterprise. Cyber Host Africa is not just a service provider — we’re your tech growth partner.
