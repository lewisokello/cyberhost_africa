About Us

Our Story

Cyber Host Technologies started with a simple but powerful vision: to bridge the digital divide in Kenya. Founded by Lewis Okello, a passionate tech innovator, we focus on providing affordable internet, software solutions, and tech education to underserved communities and forward-looking organizations.

What began as a small initiative to connect rural schools has grown into a comprehensive technology company serving communities, businesses, and institutions across Kenya.
