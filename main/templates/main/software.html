Success Story: Agricultural Supply Chain Solution

Transforming Operations for Kisumu Farmers Cooperative

We developed a custom inventory and supply chain management system for a cooperative of 1,200 small-scale farmers in Kisumu County. The solution includes:

Key Features

  • Mobile app with offline capabilities for field agents to record crop data
  • SMS-based price alerts and payment notifications for farmers without smartphones
  • Web dashboard for cooperative management to track inventory and plan logistics
  • Integration with M-Pesa for seamless mobile payments to farmers

Reduction in post-harvest losses

This system has revolutionized how we operate. Before, we were using paper records and losing track of inventory. Now everything is digitized, our farmers get paid faster, and we can plan our operations with real data. The fact that it works even without internet in some areas makes it perfect for our needs.
