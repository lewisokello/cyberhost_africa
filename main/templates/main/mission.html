Our Mission

Empowering communities through technology and connectivity

Our Mission Statement

Empowering underserved communities across Kenya with reliable internet access, hands-on technical support, future-ready AI training, and powerful digital tools — because no one should be left behind in the digital age.

At Cyber Host Technologies, we believe that access to technology and the internet is not a luxury but a necessity in today's world. Our mission drives everything we do, from connecting remote villages to training the next generation of tech innovators.

We've developed a sustainable business model that allows us to deliver cutting-edge technology solutions while maintaining our commitment to social impact. By serving both commercial clients and underserved communities, we create a virtuous cycle where business success fuels our mission-driven initiatives.

Learn More About Us

Our Vision

A connected Kenya where rural and urban communities have equal access to digital tools and opportunities, positioning the country as a leader in inclusive technological development.

We envision a future where location is no longer a barrier to digital inclusion, where every Kenyan has the tools and skills to participate in the global digital economy, and where technology serves as a bridge rather than a divide.

By 2030, we aim to have connected over 1,000 rural communities, trained 100,000 students in digital skills, and helped establish Kenya as a model for inclusive technological development that other African nations can follow.

Our Impact Goals for 2025-2030

Connect 1,000+ Rural Communities

Bring reliable internet to over 1,000 underserved communities across Kenya by 2030, starting with 100 by the end of 2025.

Current progress: 52 communities connected

Train 100,000 Students

Provide AI and coding education to 100,000 students in rural and urban schools through our proprietary learning platform.

Current progress: 5,200 students enrolled

Support 5,000 Local Businesses

Help 5,000 local businesses digitize their operations, access new markets, and increase revenues through technology adoption.

Current progress: 217 businesses supported

Create 2,000 Tech Jobs

Generate 2,000 new technology jobs in underserved communities through direct employment and skills development.

Current progress: 83 jobs created
