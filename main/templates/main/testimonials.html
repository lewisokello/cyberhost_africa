Principal, Kitui County Secondary School

Community Impact

Our services have reached over 50 communities across Kenya, providing internet access to more than 20,000 people who previously had limited or no connectivity. Through our educational programs, we've trained over 5,000 students in various technology skills, helping to bridge the digital divide and create new opportunities.

We're proud to be part of Kenya's digital transformation and remain committed to expanding our reach to more underserved areas.
