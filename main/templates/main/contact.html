Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and solutions

How quickly can you set up internet in a rural area?

Typically, we can complete a Starlink installation within 1-2 weeks of confirming your order, depending on your location and specific requirements. Our team will conduct a site assessment first to ensure optimal placement and connectivity.

What areas do you currently serve?

We primarily serve Kenya, with a focus on Nairobi, Kisumu and Mombasa County. However, we can provide certain services to neighboring countries and are continuously expanding our coverage area. Please contact us to discuss your specific location needs.

How does your LMS work in areas with limited internet?

Our Learning Management System is specially designed for low-bandwidth environments with offline capabilities, content caching, and optimized media delivery. Students can download lessons when connected and continue learning offline, with progress syncing when they reconnect.

What types of businesses do you typically work with?

We work with a wide range of clients including schools, small businesses, NGOs, healthcare facilities, and agricultural enterprises. Our solutions are scalable and can be customized to meet the needs of organizations of all sizes and sectors.

Do you offer payment plans for your services?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including monthly subscriptions and installment plans for hardware purchases. We're committed to making technology accessible and can work with you to find a payment structure that fits your budget.
